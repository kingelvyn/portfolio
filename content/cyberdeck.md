# Cyberdeck v0.11 WIP

---

### Summary
> The purpose of this project is to create a handheld or portable computing platform that combines engineering utility with cybersecurity learning. It should be capable of supporting real technical workflows such as serial communication, GPIO interfacing, embedded development, packet analysis, scripting, and field diagnostics while remaining compact enough for transport and off-grid operation.
